Cloud Infrastructure Migration & Modernization

Case Study: Enterprise Transformation

Discover how Assured IT Global delivered a seamless migration to cloud infrastructure for a Fortune 500 client, improving scalability, security, and reducing costs by 40%. Our end-to-end solution included virtualization, network overhaul, and advanced cybersecurity.

Project Highlights

  • Client: Fortune 500 Enterprise
  • Scope: 200+ servers, 500+ workstations
  • Solution: Cloud migration, virtualization, network security, disaster recovery
  • Outcome: 99.9% uptime, 40% cost reduction, improved security

Our team designed and implemented a modern IT infrastructure, migrating legacy systems to a secure cloud platform. The project included deploying enterprise-grade firewalls, endpoint protection, and automated disaster recovery, resulting in improved operational efficiency and business continuity.

"Assured IT Global delivered a seamless migration and robust security, enabling us to scale and innovate faster than ever."

Our client now enjoys improved reliability, security, and cost savings, with a future-ready IT infrastructure that supports ongoing growth and innovation.
